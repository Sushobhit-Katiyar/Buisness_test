GeM Order Cancellation Guide

How to cancel an order on the Government e-Marketplace

For Buyers:

  • 📣 Log in to your GeM portal.
  • 📣 Go to Dashboard > Orders.
  • 📣 Click the order number to open details.
  • 📣 Select Request for Order Cancellation.
  • 📣 Provide a valid reason and submit via OTP verification.

For Sellers:

  • 📣 Log in to GeM.
  • 📣 Navigate to Dashboard > Orders.
  • 📣 Select the order and choose to cancel.
  • 📣 Enter your cancellation reason and confirm with OTP.

📋 GeM Cancellation Policy Highlights:

  • 📣 Orders can only be canceled before dispatch or service initiation.
  • 📣 Use the cancellation feature responsibly; repeated misuse may attract penalties.
  • 📣 Ensure timely communication and documentation for smooth cancellation.

WHAT IS GEM PORTAL?

Complete guide to Government e-Marketplace

The Government e-Marketplace (GeM) is India's largest online platform for government procurement, connecting government buyers with verified sellers across the country. Launched in 2016 and operationalized in 2017, GeM has transformed how Indian government organizations purchase goods and services, promoting transparency, efficiency, and cost-effectiveness.
